## 2. QUALIOPI 2026 CHANGES

### Major Regulatory Shifts

**Extension of mandatory certification**: Starting 2026, all training organizations preparing for professional certification must be Qualiopi-certified, even without public funding. The FAFCEA now mandates this -- from July 1, 2026, no Qualiopi = no funding.

**New V10 referential**: Expected in 2026, the V10 guide de lecture will bring substantial changes including:
- At least one new indicator specific to apprenticeship
- Mandatory accessibility engagement register
- Revised audit modalities

**Reinforced audit requirements**:
- Mandatory presence of the organization director during audit (no more delegation to external consultants)
- Audits can be unannounced and delegated to third parties
- Shift from documentary compliance to operational proof -- Qualiopi is becoming a management tool, not just a checkbox

**Control priorities (Circular DGEFP/MOC/2026/30, February 17, 2026)**:
- CPF and apprenticeship must represent at minimum 75% of all controls
- Fraud detection is a top priority, especially in Ile-de-France
- Sanctions include: mise en demeure (30 days minimum), removal from RNCP/RS registries, and up to 2-year ban on new certification submissions

### What This Means for Pierre

This is perhaps the single highest-value positioning opportunity. Pierre:
- Has direct experience preparing and passing Qualiopi audits across multiple campuses
- Understands the new referential requirements from inside (he lives them daily)
- Can speak to the "director must be present" rule from the perspective of someone who works with directors

## 4. RNCP CERTIFICATION LANDSCAPE CHANGES

### Key Regulatory Developments

**Decree 2025-500 (June 6, 2025)** -- major reform of professional certification:
- Mandatory professional insertion data for ALL cohorts (including RS certifications)
- First-time RNCP/RS registration with a single cohort gets 3 years instead of 5
- Strengthened control and sanction mechanisms for certifying bodies

**Vademecum 2026 (January 21, 2026)** -- France Competences published 36 practical fact sheets covering the entire RNCP/RS registration pathway. This is the new reference document for anyone filing certification dossiers.

**New enrichment requirements**: RNCP certifications must now include competency blocks addressing:
- Ecological transition
- Digital transition
- Accessibility
- Workplace health and safety

### France Competences Control Capabilities

Controls can now be:
- Conducted on documents (pièces) -- France Competences can request any document
- Delegated to third-party mandated auditors
- Conducted without prior warning (inopine)

Sanctions for non-compliance:
- Mise en demeure with 30-day minimum compliance deadline
- Removal from RNCP/RS registries
- Up to 2-year ban on new certification submissions

### What This Means for Pierre

Pierre's Gradly tool was built precisely to solve the pain point of RNCP compliance documentation. The new Vademecum's 36 fact sheets, combined with stricter enforcement, will drive demand for:
- Expert guidance on navigating the new requirements
- Tools that automate compliance evidence generation
- Consulting on competency block design that meets new enrichment criteria

### Actionable Content Angles

- Blog (already planned for June 1): "Blocs de competences RNCP : les concevoir, les evaluer, les automatiser" -- update to reference the new Vademecum
- Blog: "Vademecum RNCP 2026 : les 36 fiches decryptees par un praticien" -- break down the official document into actionable guidance
